FAQ

Common questions before reaching out

01

Can I get help choosing between collections?

Yes, the contact path should feel like a design conversation, not only a support request. Our team is here to guide you through DAX's full assortment — from sofas and dining sets to bedroom collections — so you find exactly what suits your space and style.

02

Are the categories based on the full DAX assortment?

Yes, the marketing presentation maps directly to the dax.co.il category structure and visual sourcing. Every collection shown reflects the breadth of furniture available — from living room essentials to curated accent pieces — so what you browse is what you can order.

03

Can I ask about materials and finishes?

Absolutely. We encourage visitors to inquire about upholstery options, wood tones, lacquer finishes, and care expectations before committing. Whether you're comparing fabric grades or deciding between walnut and oak, our team provides the detail you need to choose with confidence.

04

Is showroom guidance available?

Yes. Visits and design consultations can be arranged based on your schedule and project details. Share your requirements through the contact form and we will coordinate a personalised showroom appointment — ideal for home buyers and interior design enthusiasts planning a complete room refresh.
