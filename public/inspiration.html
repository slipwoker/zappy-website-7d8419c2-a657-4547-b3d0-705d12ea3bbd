Three styling directions to guide decisions

From curated minimalism to urban luxury, DAX collections are designed to match every interior vision — whether you're refreshing a single room or furnishing an entire home.

Modern Minimal

Low clutter, defined geometry, and tightly curated contrast. Sharp silhouettes in neutral tones anchor each piece as a deliberate design statement.

Warm Natural

Wood-forward tones, soft upholstery, and welcoming texture. Materials are chosen for tactile richness and a palette that feels effortlessly at home.

Urban Luxury

Dark finishes, sculptural forms, and hotel-like sophistication. Each piece commands attention through proportion, material depth, and restrained opulence.
